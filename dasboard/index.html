Frequently Asked Questions

How do I recalibrate a soil moisture sensor?

Navigate to the Sensors view, click on the specific sensor ID, and select the 'Recalibrate' option from the action menu.

What does the 'Breakdown' health metric mean?

Breakdown refers to crops showing early signs of stress due to lack of nutrients or pests, calculated via satellite multispectral imagery.
